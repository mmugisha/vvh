Our Hotel

An Oasis of Serenity.

We invite you to experience a unique and calming stay at one of Uganda’s most charming and distinguished new hotels. Nestled in the rolling hills of Kampala, Victoria View surveys the grand Lake Victoria with an unparalleled view from above the trees. Offering a luxurious, yet comfortable environment with a touch of African flair, this boutique hotel seeks to serve the business or leisure traveler with airy, spacious rooms and a new standard of detail-oriented service.

Touring in Uganda

Our experienced team can tailor-made a tour of Uganda's beautiful scenery and wildlife just for you. We organize tours to see the mountain gorillas at Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. You can also see the tree-climbing lions, giraffes, rhinos, cheetahs and other animals at the expansive Queen Elizabeth National Park. You can also see the powerful Nile River or visit the thunderous Murchison's Fall in North-West Uganda.

HOTEL AMENITIES


Breathtaking Views, Delicious Food & Comfy Rooms.

ROOMS / ACCOMMODATION

All of our very spacious, modern rooms in the estate overlook the serene waters of Lake Victoria – survey vistas of painted sunsets and take in the cool breeze on your private terrace.

Revel in luxury while enjoying en suite bathrooms with marble vanities in each room, ample built-in storage, luxurious linens, and floor-to-ceiling windows. Choose the perfect setup for yourself or family – our rooms vary in size with twin, queen or king size beds.

AIRPORT PICKUP & CAR HIRE

Let us arrange for you a stress-free pick-up or drop-off at Entebbe International Airport from one of our professional drivers. Our clean, comfortable vans or 4x4 can be hired to tour the sights and sounds of Kampala or explore the wondrous,breathtaking forests / parks of Uganda.

BUSINESS

Free, fast internet is available via Wi-Fi to all of our business travelers. Catch up on emails in the comfort of your room or in our lounge area. Schedule a ride from your door to your afternoon meeting; or bring the meeting to us – Our Grand Room is a perfect setting for the offsite conference call or meeting.

GYM & FITNESS

Unwind and recharge in our private fitness centre. Featuring cycling machines, treadmill, weights and more to meet your workout needs. If the green outdoors is calling, steep running tracks flow through the lushly landscaped hills waiting to challenge your stamina.

EXPLORE UGANDA - Safaris & more...


Experience the wonders of awe-inspiring Uganda. Whether it's trekking to locate the mountain gorillas, safari in the national parks, or a tour of colorful Kampala; we will make your experience memorable.

Queen Elizabeth national park

Be one of the few to realize the vast ecosystem of Queen Elizabeth National Park, resting against the jagged peaks of the Rwenzori Mountains. See some of the 600+ species of birds as well as tree-climbing lions, giraffes, buffalo, rhinos, cheetahs at what has been designated a Biosphere Reserve for Humanity under UNESCO auspices.

Bwindi Impenetrable

Those seeking a mix of tranquility and adventure can trekk some of Uganda’s most revered emerald green forests – Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. Over 25,000 years old, this rare and fascinating forest, hosts over half of the world’s population of gorillas.

Murchison's Fall on River Nile

It’s difficult to deny the allure of thunderous power and severity of size from the world-famous Nile River. Get active with various watersports and enjoy waterside restaurants in Jinja. Or visit the Nile’s tumultuous side and get an unobstructed view at the massive elephants near Murchison's Falls in North-West Uganda.

Directions


We organize shuttles from Entebbe to Victoria View Boutique Hotel upon request.

From Entebbe

The drive from Entebbe International Airport to Victoria View Boutique Hotel generally takes 30 min to 1 hr. This Estate is on Kigo road off Entebbe Road. Kigo Road will be on your right and is located between Kajjansi and Lweza, towns that taxi drivers are familiar with which leads to Lake Victoria Serena Hotel.

From Kampala

The Estate is on Kigo road off Entebbe Road. If you're coming from Kampala, Kigo road will be just past the 'Fresh Cuts' meat factory and immediately after the Fuelex gas station. Take an immediate left onto Kigo road (towards Lake Victoria Serena Hotel, a known landmark).
